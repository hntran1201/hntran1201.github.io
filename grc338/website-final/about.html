At Da Vien Coffee, our story begins with a deep passion for authentic Vietnamese coffee and the cultural traditions that shaped it. Rooted in Vietnam’s long-standing heritage of robust flavors and slow-brewed enjoyment, our mission has always been to honor these origins while sharing them with a wider community here in Southern California and beyond. Every cup we serve is a reflection of the care, precision, and reverence we hold for Vietnamese coffee culture.

Our name, Da Vien, translates to “ice cube” in Vietnamese—a symbol of the refreshing essence we infuse into each experience. Whether you’re savoring a bold cà phê sữa đá or exploring one of our signature specialty drinks, we strive to evoke the same invigorating, cool clarity that an ice cube brings to a perfect brew. This identity guides everything we do, from ingredient selection to our approach in crafting drinks that awaken the senses.

Over the years, we’ve dedicated ourselves to meticulously creating blends that stay true to their origins while elevating the flavors unique to Vietnamese coffee. Our beans, brewing methods, and recipes reflect a harmony of tradition and innovation, ensuring every sip carries both authenticity and modern-day creativity. We take pride in offering drinks that resonate with those familiar with Vietnamese coffee as well as newcomers discovering its depth for the first time.

Located in the heart of Southern California, Da Vien Coffee is more than just a café—it’s a community space for connection, comfort, and exploration. We’ve cultivated an atmosphere where guests can slow down, enjoy a thoughtfully crafted drink, and share in the vibrant culture that inspires us. Whether you’re here to work, relax, or simply indulge, our goal is to provide an experience as refreshing and memorable as our name implies.
